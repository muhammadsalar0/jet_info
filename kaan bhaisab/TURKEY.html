Thruster

The Kaan's maiden warplane (also known as the TF Kaan or Milli Muharip Uçak) is powered by two General Electric F110-GE-129 turbofan engines, each producing around 29,000 pounds of thrust. These powerful engines give the aircraft supersonic speed, advanced maneuverability, and the ability to supercruise—fly faster than the speed of sound without using afterburners. In the future, the Kaan is expected to be equipped with indigenously developed engines (TEI TF35000), further enhancing Turkey’s defense independence and boosting the aircraft’s stealth and performance.
